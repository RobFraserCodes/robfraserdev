### Moodiv8

**Project Overview:**  
Moodiv8 is an innovative social media platform designed to promote positive interactions and well-being. My involvement encompassed UX design, UI design, and branding, creating a cohesive and appealing experience across mobile devices.

**Key Contributions:**
- **Branding:** Developed the brand identity, including the logo, color scheme, and typography that convey positivity and inclusivity.
- **User Experience Design:** Created a unique content discovery experience that encourages positive interactions among users. Integrated features like mood-based content filtering and engagement prompts.
- **User Interface Design:** Designed a visually appealing interface that supports easy navigation and enhances user engagement with personalized content and interactive elements.

**Impact:**  
Moodiv8 has been recognized for its user-centric design, receiving positive feedback for its focus on mental wellness and community building. The app has seen a 50% increase in user engagement since launch.
